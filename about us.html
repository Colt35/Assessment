We sell affordable seats, chairs, beds and much other furniture of your choice that you may like. We do delivery in every place across the country. Furniture Palace Kenya also offers a discount in every furniture bought. The company is trusted in giving good quality that will stay years to come.
The journey of Furniture Palace began in October 2002, with a modest showroom of 3,500 sq. ft. located in the bustling CBD of Nairobi. Founded with a belief that furniture can be sold in a better way. We have grown today to be Kenya's largest furniture retailer with 7 furniture stores in 3 major cities of Kenya; Nairobi, Mombasa, and Eldoret showcasing the widest range of home furniture, office furniture, outdoor furniture and home decor. The company continues to grow, maintaining the standards of service, integrity and dedication established as the years rolled by. Proudly employing over 200 employees within our network of corporate and franchise stores through Kenya...
